INSTITUTE DEVELOPEMENT PROJECT
(Livelihood Project)

to deliver an innovative, community-safe and beneficial program
which serves the community and its people

Problem Statement

The issue in the institution is the lack of resources like food as well as the need for greenery added to their facility. For the children, we want to address the issue of the need for psychological assessment to the children. With this in mind, our goal is to manage effectively their troubles with the children and the institution’s facility. In order to achieve that, we would come up with a proposed project solution.

Problem Description

SMAC sister is an institution that provides for children that are experiencing neglect and abandonment. In line with this, SMAC is experiencing a shortage of resources and a lack of green spaces. On the other hand, the children are in need of phycological assessments. The environment of SMAC would affect the well-being of the Institution’s members and the children living in SMAC. Their food availability would also be put at risk seeing as they are in scarcity. Additionally, they are unable to pay for the psychological assessments since it is too expensive. Not only just for one session but all the way up until the child is healed. Furthermore, several of the kids in SMAC lack prior childhood experience. With the aforementioned issues, we strive to provide a livelihood program for SMAC and its children. It would cater to their needs and address their issues. We would lead hand in hand to do our best and deliver quality assistance for their benefit.

Project summary

The problem of the partner institution is the lack of resources and the lack for a sense of childhood of the children. The lack of resources may be due to the scarcity of gardening or greenery activities. While the lack for a sense of childhood of the children may be due to the past experiences of the children as they were neglected, abandoned, or orphaned.

This project kept in mind the mission of the SMAC institution, where it aims to improve the lives of the children with traumatic past experiences and give them a home with love and care. This project aims to address the identified issues with proposed efficient project services.

First is the composting service, this helps the institution to have a sustainable food source and to have a greener environment for the community. This composting service, with its greenery that is wished to achieve, can help create a sacred space for the children for prayers, and etc. The second proposed service is a counseling assessment, where the volunteers would have a meaningful conversation and activities with the children to help with their development as they lack a sense of childhood. This service is also to help them with their personal issues, traumas, and etc.

With the planned project services, it is anticipated in the future that the institution will have a better environment. As it is hoped that the institution would have a sustainable and healthy food source to add more greenery in their facility. As for the children, it is hoped that the services would help them develop and understand themselves for the future ahead of them.

Project Background

The SMAC institution’s mission is to improve the lives of neglected, abandoned, and orphaned children and to give them a home, solving this problem would help achieve that. As we aim to solve the lack of resources in the institution and the lack of sense of childhood in the children. The problem of the children, started when the children were neglected, abandoned, and orphaned, resulting in their lack of sense of childhood. As for the problem of the institution, we saw that the facility lacks greenery and the institution needs sustainable food.

Proposed Project

The well-being of the institution and its children is our utmost priority. Our proposal for a Livelihood Program for SMAC will provide an efficient solution to address the issues and needs of the institution. For SMAC institution, we will execute a composting service and cater to their need for more supply of food and green spaces. At the same time, we will deliver a counselling/guidance program for the children where we will conduct instructive conversations that will broaden our understanding of their needs. Along with this we will try and seek out professional psychologists in volunteering for free sessions for the children to be mindful of their issues and help them develop their well-being. Since there are limitations to this project, it will cater up until the school year. It will also be highly voluntary so all the resources we need will seek out willing donors or providers. To ensure the long-term effects of this program, we will gather volunteers and build a group of members that are inclined to continue this program. We will advertise and send out publication materials that will ensure others join and help SMAC institutions.

Solution

With the aforementioned problem, we would like to deliver a livelihood program. It serves as a foundation for SMAC and its children. Our livelihood program would deliver several services that can address the problem such as a composting service and counseling assessments. This assures immediate effectivity because of how this program would project a straightforward initiative to help and address the needs of SMAC. And our livelihood program can also influence others to show their assistance and help. In terms of alternatives, this solution is shown as more efficient because it would guarantee effectivity, long terms effects, and propert application in addressing the problem. All of the plans for the program would take time and effort as any other program do, but with the members of our group, willing associates and donors, and the guidance of our moderators these factors can assist in making this program a success.

Rationale

The project delivers an innovative, community-safe, and beneficial program that addresses the needs and issues of the community. It is vital because of its initiative to provide a green space for the institution to contribute to its resources and assist in the wellness and biodiversity of our communities. As well as a sacred space for equipping their spiritual needs, using for the solace of prayer and supplication. Additionally, we aim to conduct healthy conversational assessments for the children in SMAC. This would deliver a widespread assessment that would reach the capacity of every child in their residence. Aiming for a healthy mental condition we propose this program to deliver conversational advice, webinars, as well as one on one discourse. This would help in the development of the children and how it deals with their issues and past traumas.

Objectives

Our primary objective is to provide a safe, green space for the children that would cater to their spiritual, mental, physical, emotional and social n eeds.

    The specified points of this project are the following:
  1. To deliver a composting program.
  2. Renovate a sacred space
  3. Construct green space all throughout the institution
  4. Evaluate the condition of the children
  5. Provide an assessment program for all the children
  6. Succeed in establishing support for SMAC

1. Why is the project important for the institution?

The project is important for the institution because the facility was going through a financial crisis and not being able to provide the necessary services for the children who are living there and it might cause the children to suffer more not being able to get the basic services they need and for the institution will continue to help the poor children as long as we will support them and help them provide the necessary resources.

2. Why is the project important for the ateneo students?

The students at Ateneo should take part in this research since it will boost their own mental health and wellbeing. The students will understand more and think more deeply as a result of this. Additionally, this project does not only benefit the institution but also the students of AdDu.

3. Why is the project important for the public in general?

As the project aims to assist the SMAC institution with the services that it offers, the project is important for the public in general. This project can encourage the public to volunteer and help the people in the institution. Additionally, it can also inspire them to have a project of their own to help other people.
