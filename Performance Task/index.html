SMAC is a CHILD CARE INSTITUTION that focuses on caring for
abused, neglected, and abandoned children.

The institution places a priority on educating the children to be better in their life. Giving the kids psychological and physical assistance is another goal of the organization. Up to this point, the SMAC Sisters Institution has accepted children in need and provided them with assistance and guidance to develop into artistic individuals. The Sorelle Missionarie dell'Amore di Cristo, Inc. which is SMAC, have direct experience of the struggles faced by several families, including those with children, to provide for their basic needs which got them to formally establish the SMAC Institution on Saint Joseph's Day. The young children who are subjected to violence and sexual abuse are those who are living in subpar conditions on the streets, suffering from starvation, poor cleanliness, and low morale are being taken care by the institute. The goal of SMAC is to improve the standard of care given to those kids by reorganizing its programs and formulating its services in order to set up the center as a stand-alone institution.
